About Us

Discover Our Story

Namaste & Welcome to Khana Khazana

At Khana Khazana, we take pride in celebrating the rich and diverse heritage of Indian cuisine. Our passion lies in bringing authentic flavors, vibrant spices, and time-honored cooking techniques to food lovers worldwide. True to our name, which means “Treasure of Food,” we are committed to crafting exceptional vegetarian dishes that honor tradition while embracing innovation.

Our Culinary Visionaries

The creative minds behind Khana Khazana, Chef Abhishek Patel and Chef Daxesh Shah, are culinary artisans dedicated to showcasing the essence of Indian flavors. With years of expertise in the art of cooking, they bring an unparalleled level of craftsmanship to every dish.

Under their leadership, our kitchen is a space where traditional recipes are thoughtfully reimagined with contemporary finesse. Their passion for Indian cuisine drives them to innovate while preserving the authenticity that makes every meal at Khana Khazana a truly unique and unforgettable dining experience.

Join Us for a Culinary Adventure!

Step into Khana Khazana and indulge in a feast of flavors that take you on a journey across India. Whether you’re craving the richness of creamy Paneer Butter Masala, the comforting warmth of Dal Tadka, or the crispy goodness of Masala Dosas, we have something for everyone.

We look forward to serving you and making every meal a cherished memory. Namaste & Happy Dining!

Our Vision

Spreading the Magic of Indian Vegetarian Cuisine.
Khana Khazana aims to be the USA’s top vegetarian Indian restaurant, blending authenticity, innovation, and passion in every dish.

Our Mission

Redefining Indian Vegetarian Dining.
At Khana Khazana, our mission is to bring the diverse flavors of India to every plate while maintaining authenticity, freshness, and innovation.

A Commitment to Authenticity & Excellence

Every dish at Khana Khazana is meticulously crafted using the freshest ingredients, hand-selected spices, and a deep understanding of flavors. Our culinary philosophy is rooted in quality, authenticity, and creativity, ensuring that every meal is not only a delight to the taste buds but also a nourishing experience for the soul. Our dedication to excellence allows us to present dishes that harmoniously blend tradition with modern innovation, offering a culinary journey like no other.

A Journey Through Flavors

At Khana Khazana, we invite you on a gastronomic adventure where every bite tells a story, every flavor transports you to the heart of India, and every dish is a masterpiece. Join us in experiencing the magic of Indian cuisine—where tradition and creativity come together to create an extraordinary celebration of taste.
